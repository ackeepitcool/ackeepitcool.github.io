  • #FilmsNotDead

  • Having moved coast-to-coast twice now (North Carolina to California, California to New York), and having driven both times, I have seen more of the United States by road than most people see in their lifetime. As such, I wanted a way to make my journey impactful. I had my digital SLR, sure, but I decided to start another personal project to help myself grow as a creative: I wanted to capture my journey through film. Film is an unforgiving medium. You have one shot to make it count. As such, I decided I was going to take this time to document my life in film, to see it as it is: you only get one shot at life. Make it count.
